The most important part of any bed is the frame. It can be made of solid wood, metal, chipboard or MDF. All of these materials are strong enough to support the weight of one or two adults. The choice depends on personal preference, as well as the style in which the bedroom is decorated.

You can find a bed with an orthopedic base. The presence of a lifting mechanism and linen boxes will be a pleasant bonus.

In a classic interior, massive wooden beds will look good, as well as models with fabric upholstery of the frame and headboard. For boho, country and Provence, options with a metal frame are suitable. For a modern or minimalist interior, the optimal solution would be a laconic model made of chipboard or MDF.

It is important to find a balance between commonplace functionality and the aesthetic side of the beds, as well as determine the size and shape.
