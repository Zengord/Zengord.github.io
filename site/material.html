Mattress materials

The production of mattresses is carried out from safe natural and artificial materials:

Latex is a natural filler for premium models. Sisal is a coir of agave leaves, which gives elasticity and an orthopedic effect to the product. Coconut fiber is a durable and hygienic filler for rigid models. Foam is a resilient, porous material made from polyester compounds.

To impart special properties to mattresses in multilayer inner filling, innovative materials are used. They have the effect of thermoregulation or memory, antiseptic properties, and increased strength.
