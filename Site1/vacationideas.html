Ideas for tourism

Hot Place

This town is really full of energy, noise, fun of life! It is not easy to say what attracts tourists more: wonderful pictures of nature, luxurious conditions for beach and intense entertainment, magnificent hotels, spas, nightclubs, or historical monuments.

Island

There are ensembles with radon and mineral springs, a wide network of tourist routes in the mountainous area and along the coast, wonderful highways, picturesque geographical points of interest, ethnographic villages of the population of the island of nationalities, and the key is ecological cleanliness and easy transport accessibility.

Fortress

From a wide variety of recreation options, you can choose the one that best suits your taste preferences and availability. Availability for recreation here is much higher than similar holidays abroad, and the level of service and infrastructure is as close as possible to the needs of a comfortable stay for guests of the capital.

Rocky island

Important in the center of a warm bay, surrounded by mountains and pompous beaches, overgrown with virgin jungle, the peninsula impresses not only with its beauty, but also with volumes that are absolutely comparable to the adjacent land. In recent years, the tourism infrastructure here has been developing at a faster pace: an airport, dozens of casinos, hotels, fresh roads, equipped beaches, golf courses were noticed.
