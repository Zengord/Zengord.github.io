Computer chairs and chairs

A specially designed computer chair or chair helps to improve working conditions, which makes it possible to distribute the load on the seated spine in different ways.

The choice of a computer chair should be based on the duration of its continuous use:

up to 2 hours - a minimum set of settings, a model made of inexpensive materials;
from 3 to 5 hours - an office chair for a computer should be equipped with manual adjustment of the height and position of the back, it would be pertinent to think about a full-fledged workplace;
from 5 hours - such a long stay in place should be compensated for by a good chair with an automatic seat and backrest adjustment function, which will adjust in accordance with the sitting posture.
