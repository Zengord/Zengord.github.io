Junior group

What tasks are we solving

The program for the youngest provides students with a safe and vibrant language environment
in which they can learn and develop. We pay great attention to the harmonious adaptation of the child in the environment and socialization skills.

What skills are we developing

• We form a vocabulary on everyday topics;
• Develop coordination, fine and large motor skills;
• We teach you to understand yourself and interact with the world around you.

Middle group

What tasks are we solving

With the application of the international early learning program for reading and writing, children get acquainted with sounds in a playful, innovative way and acquire phonetic language skills.

What skills are we developing

• Native language: phonetic skills, literacy training, vocabulary enrichment, reading longer and more complex words;
• Math block: score up to 100;
addition and subtraction within 10;
• Development of logical and creative thinking.

Why you should attend classes

Additional education in kindergarten, along with general, contributes to the versatile development of personality, therefore it is important for the child. From an early age, children learn:

navigate the socio-cultural environment;
adapt to society;
organize your leisure and free time;
to form cognitive motivation;
enrich sensory experience;
gain new knowledge about surrounding objects and phenomena.
The task of parents is to direct the child's attention in time to the study of the area that is attractive to him. Many fear that the extra load tires children, reduces productivity and slows down development. However, with an adequate approach and a moderate number of lessons, the child will not get tired and will be happy to go to exciting classes.
