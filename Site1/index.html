with energy-saving, halogen, LED lamps;

with a fan or a control panel (dimmer), etc. Expert recommendations for choosing:

It is important to choose a model that will fully meet your needs in terms of color or style. There are a number of rules when choosing:

the chosen model must correspond to the area of ​​\u200b\u200bthe room, in harmony with the furniture. For example, a giant chandelier will be combined with a powerful sofa, for low ceilings, than any other, choose small options;
regulation of brightness, power and the likelihood of dimming will be an added plus;
make sure that the ceilings will withstand the authority of the lamp;
When choosing chandeliers, do not forget about energy efficiency. LED technologies are now listed as the most economical option;
pay attention to the image of the lamp. In addition to LEDs, there are classic incandescent lamps. They consume a large amount of energy, but until now they are often used because of their own saturated light, and halogen ones stand out for their unsurpassed color rendering and long service life;
cartridges differ not only in diameter, but also in material. Often you see ceramics and carbolite (resistant to fire), and also plastic (suitable for lamps, the bases of which are slightly heated).
