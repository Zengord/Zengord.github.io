Selection criterion

In order not to forget anything and not get lost in the variety of choice of a wide assortment, you need to focus on the main criteria:

Form. Elegant circle or heart shapes always look attractive, but practice shows. that they are not so easy to use. The most standard rectangular beds, something that is time-tested, can be said to be a classic. If the soul still licks for an unusual variation, then it is best to pay attention to the wardrobe bed. So you will be able to save maximum space and provide the most comfortable sleeping place.

Dimensions. In addition to the standard divisions into one, one and a half and double beds, there are various individual criteria for the optimal size in terms of height, length and width. To calculate the necessary comfortable length, it is recommended to add at least 10-15 cm to your height. The width will become equal to the distance from elbow to elbow between arms spread apart plus 20 centimeters. The selection of the height is carried out strictly in accordance with personal preferences. If it is very difficult to find the necessary dimensions, you can always consider an individual bed for production. This will give you confidence that the bed will meet the parameters you expect.

Frame. The estimate of the quality of the frame depends on the expected load. For the same single bed, the frame can be made of their chipboard; with excellent assembly, the model can last a very long time. Double beds are designed for heavy loads, therefore only durable materials such as wood and matell are provided in them.

Base. A popular option is a base made of a solid frame with flexible lamellas laid on it. It perfectly withstands the load and retains its shape for a long time. Additionally, it is possible to equip a storage system with a lifting mechanism or drawers in the base.

Mattress. The most important part of a comfortable sleeping place, which directly affects the quality of sleep. It all depends on the possible load and personal preferences for rigidity and softness.
