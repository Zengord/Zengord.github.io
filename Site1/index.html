All you need to know about yacht charter

Driving a yacht, like any other rental vehicle, urgently requires specific practical abilities, which must be proven by an official document. Only holders of a special skipper's license are allowed to steer the vessel, which is issued upon completion of the yachting secondary schools in case of successful passing of the exam. There are a number of types of maritime licenses, and charter firms in various states have every chance of having all sorts of requests for them. The majority of sailors use one of the proper skipper certificates for charter:

A license is an international "right" to operate a ship, allowing you to sail in coastal waters. Practice has proven that, in fact, domestic yachtsmen in most cases choose this certificate, for example, as it is recognized all over the world. If you are interested in booking a boat with a charter company, then this license will literally not hurt you!
The certificate is a license to operate small boats, which is more suitable than any other for swimming in inland waters. Not least, some foreign charter firms accept them.
A certificate is a certificate that only allows you to swim during the day. The study is conducted according to the English system, and upon completion you will be able to reserve a vessel in literally any country in the world. If you do not have a license yet or you are the first to discover yachting for yourself, you will still be able to go on a cruise, but accompanied by a skilled licensed “captain”. In any case, chartering with a skipper will be quite an enriching skill for those who wish to master the practical basics of steering and mooring a boat. Be aware that the skipper's offers are paid separately from the rental, and in fact that in most cases he will need a separate cabin on your yacht.

Mooring in a special place

During your charter you will make stops at special stops. This is the main substance of the yachting infrastructure, where you can moor for the night, replenish supplies of water and fuel, take a shower, and even replenish food. Mooring in marinas, as a rule, is not included in the price of renting a vessel and is paid separately in space. The price of parking has the ability to strongly vary depending on the volume of the boat and the time of year. In the high season, prices rise strongly, for example, what space than any other book in advance. Boats are moored to the pier with the stern, side or bow. This complex maneuver must be performed by an experienced skipper with the support of an assistant, who, in the given case, will be a marina worker. When mooring a yacht, it is imperative to take into account the purpose of the wind and current, speed, the condition of the fenders, the length and tension of the mooring lines.
