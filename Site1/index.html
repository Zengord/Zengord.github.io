How to choose a boat?

Dimensions of the vessel. Small models reach 4 and a half meters in length. They are used in the presenter for fishing in the pond. These boats are quite maneuverable, easy to control and do not waste a lot of fuel for 1 trip. There are also more large-sized options with a length of 6 and a half and 8 meters. They are suitable for large companies or family trips. Motor power. The speed of movement and the likelihood of transporting something in tow depends on it. The number of horses also affects the fuel consumption and, as a result, the rental price. Normal boats for fishermen are equipped with engines with 90 horses, but pleasure boats and boats for towing wakeboarders can carry up to 350 horses. The capacity directly affects the usability of the boat. The more spacious the vessel is, the more convenient it will be for a company of vacationers. Small models can accommodate up to 3 people, and more serious options will be taken on board up to 8 passengers. Purpose and accessories. There are boats for slow walks along the colorful corners of the Ivankovskoye reservoir or the Volga. There are boats for wakeboarding, towing aqua skis and wakesurfing. And there are boats that are impeccably favorable for fishermen. The equipment is subject to the buyer's requests and the vessel's applications.
