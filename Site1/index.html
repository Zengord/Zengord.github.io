How to choose a sports trainer

When choosing the best option, several factors should be taken into account

1

Trainer type

perhaps a treadmill, an elliptical or exercise bike, a stepper, or a versatile device. It is important to understand what type of load you need, the volume and what kind of workouts are planned in the future.

2

Foldable body and dimensions

when selecting, the size of the simulator for a small apartment or room is very important.

3

Number of programs

many models can be designed for two or three tasks, while others can offer a complete workout that consists of many different exercises and loads.

4

Power consumption

with frequent use, it is worth paying attention to such an important part as power consumption.

5

Additional functions

The presence of a heart rate monitor and other functions that will be useful for you to monitor your condition - this aspect is very important when training at home for safe and effective exercise.
