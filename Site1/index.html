Confectionery for the holiday

Would you like to decorate a birthday, anniversary or corporate party? Arrange solemn moments fun and unforgettable? The original cake, made in a single copy according to your order, will immediately be a decoration of fun and the hero of countless photos!

You can choose a cake from a sketch created by our confectioners, or you can deliver a personal sketch - and we will turn your imagination into delicious reality. The smallest authority - 1 kg. In your order: marzipan cover, sweet handmade figures, photographic images on a confectionery film, an arabesque of berries and exotic fruits. All cakes can be arranged in two tiers in agreement with the pastry chef. We work with different types of fillings and insides: yoghurt, sour cream, fruit, cheesecake and bozhassi, tiramisu and honey cake. The shortest time for accepting an order for making a cake with a marzipan cover is 5 days and nights in advance. The shortest time for accepting an order for making a cake without marzipan cover is 3 days and nights.
