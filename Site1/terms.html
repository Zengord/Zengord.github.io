AGREEMENT
FOR THE PROCESSING OF PERSONAL DATA OF THE CLIENT
An individual (hereinafter referred to as the Personal Data Subject), by sending his personal data through the form on the website or in the mobile application (hereinafter referred to as the System), gives his consent to the Operator for their processing (hereinafter referred to as the Consent) on the conditions set out below:
The subject of personal data confirms that he is a capable natural person and transfers his personal data to the Operator freely, without coercion, at his own will and in his interest.
LIST OF PERSONAL DATA, for the processing of which Consent is given: personal data: surname, name, phone number, e-mail address, delivery address, passport data when sending goods or when receiving prepaid goods.
Technical information: IP address, operating system type, browser type, geographic location, Internet service provider; information about the behavior of the Subject of personal data in the System, information automatically obtained when accessing the System, including the use of cookies, data on making an online payment: about the time, method and amount of payment.
LEGAL BASIS for the processing of personal data are: Laws, Policy in the field of personal data processing; acceptance of the offer posted in the System (User Agreement, Purchase and Sale Agreement, Service Agreement).
THE PURPOSE OF THE PROCESSING OF PERSONAL DATA IS:
providing access to the System, including for concluding contracts based on offers;
identification of the Subject of personal data, including when gaining access to the Profile;
processing of Orders;
fulfillment of obligations under the Offer;
delivery of goods and transfer of goods for delivery to a transport company;
establishing and maintaining communication with the Subject of personal data, including the direction of: responses to inquiries; informational messages to a phone number and / or e-mail address: in pursuance of the Offer (order status, receipt, etc.), about promo codes for next purchases, and other information of advertising and news content; information about the operation of the System.
Ensuring the operability and security of the System (preventing cases of fraud and other abuse, as well as for investigating such cases);
Identification of System malfunctions;
Conducting statistical and marketing research on consumer demand, as well as for targeting (targeting the target audience) advertisements placed on the basis of Impersonal Personal Data;
dispute resolution;
collecting reviews;
fulfillment of the established requirements.
LIST OF ACTIONS with personal data: collection, recording, systematization, accumulation, storage, clarification (update, change), electronic copying, extraction, use, transfer (distribution, provision, access), depersonalization, blocking, deletion and destruction.
Consent is given to the processing of personal data both with the use of automation tools and without the use of such tools.
The personal data subject agrees that the Operator has the right to transfer personal data to third parties, including a transport company, a courier service for delivery, and other persons to process personal data on behalf of the Operator in order to fulfill contractual obligations and achieve the goals specified in Consent. In this case, the Operator has the right to disclose personal data to these persons to the extent necessary.
CONSENT TO RECEIVE MISSIONS OF AN ADVERTISING-INFORMATION CHARACTER. The subject of personal data, when registering in the System, placing an Order, agrees to receive mailings of an advertising and informational nature from the Operator.
SUBJECT OF PERSONAL DATA RIGHT TO unsubscribe from advertising and newsletters at any time by clicking on the link located at the end of each marketing email message, or in the Profile, unchecking the notifications and saving the new settings.
THE CONSENT IS VALID from the moment of its provision and until the moment of execution of the corresponding agreement or withdrawal of the Consent.
THE PERSONAL DATA SUBJECT IS RIGHT TO WITHDRAW CONSENT by sending an application to the Operator: in the form of an electronic document to the email address;
The application must contain information that allows you to identify the Subject of personal data, including the data of his phone number specified when placing an order or registering in the System.
