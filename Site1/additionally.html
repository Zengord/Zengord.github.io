How old can I start visiting

Additional classes in kindergarten are provided for children from 1 to 7 years old. The training is carried out by experienced teachers who find a personal approach to the wards. In general education classes in the group, some pupils are ahead of their peers, while others do not have time to master the material. Therefore, additional classes at the children's center allow the student to study the subject in accordance with his individual abilities and the pace of development.
