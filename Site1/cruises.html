The most beautiful cruises

A cruise is an opportunity to rent a separate comfortable cabin for two or to book a whole yacht with a crew. Our experts have compiled the most interesting routes for you, for example, in fact, what remains for you is just to enjoy the entertainment. You don't need a license or a skipper's skill to cruise - we'll take care of everything!

Cabin rental:

For couples in love and seekers of new adventures and acquaintances, cabin rentals are perfect. If you decide to rent a cabin on a yacht, we will help you decide which ship you will spend your vacation on. Are you a romantic and dream of sailing on a maneuverable yacht or a luxurious catamaran? Or do you want to spend your holidays on a large luxury yacht or a traditional wooden schooner? Depending on the country where you are going on a cruise, you can walk along the beaches or sightseeing tours. Perhaps you want a cabin on a yacht with a special culinary program or a cabin on a catamaran with increased comfort? All your wishes can be taken into account: the captain will create a route according to your preferences.

Family cruise:

Would you like to spend a holiday with your family or friends under sail and not think about the route, anchorages and including cooking? Would you like to present a cruise on a yacht to your loved ones? During this time, your choice is a home cruise. Crewed private cruises are great because you can completely take your mind off things. The captain will build a route according to your wishes, and the hostess will provide you with food and drinks. If desired, the skipper has the opportunity to teach you the practical basics of navigation, and you can stand behind the wheel yourself. It is possible to personalize the swimming program every time in order to take into account the requests of all members of the cruise and visit the best beaches and places of interest. Your chef will offer you personalized rations from the best regional marine products.
