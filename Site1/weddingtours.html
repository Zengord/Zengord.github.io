Wedding tours

We invite you to go on an unforgettable honeymoon trip with our travel agency. Start your family life on a beautiful note!

Wedding tours can be divided into two categories:

for couples who want to get married abroad;
for newlyweds who go on a romantic trip.

Even a married couple can repeat the ceremony against the backdrop of a snow-white beach and ocean waves, in an old castle or a beautiful church. As a rule, wedding tours include the organization of a celebration so that the lovers do not have to worry about anything.

Wedding ceremony abroad

Keep in mind that a marriage certificate issued by competent foreign authorities cannot be exchanged for another. Such a document must go through the legalization procedure. If you are planning to register a marriage abroad, we will help you prepare the necessary documents.

If you just want bright exotics and impressions, you can limit yourself to a symbolic ceremony, and upon returning to your homeland, formalize the marriage. Newlyweds most often prefer the islands. You can at least every year go on a wedding anniversary to a new corner of the world and hold a ceremony according to local customs.
