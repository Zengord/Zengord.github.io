processing of Orders;
fulfillment of obligations under the Offer;
delivery of goods and transfer of goods for delivery by a transport company;
establishing and maintaining communication with the Personal Data Subject, including sending: responses to requests; informational messages to the phone number and / or e-mail address: in pursuance of the Offer (Order status, check, etc.), about promotional codes for the next purchases, and other information of advertising and news content; information about the operation of the System.
Ensuring the operability and security of the System (to prevent cases of fraud and other abuses, as well as to investigate such cases);
Troubleshooting of the System;
Conducting statistical and marketing research of consumer demand, as well as for targeting (identification of the target audience) of advertising placed on the basis of Anonymous Personal Data;
dispute resolution;
collecting feedback;
fulfillment of established requirements.
