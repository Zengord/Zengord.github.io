Privacy Policy

GENERAL PROVISIONS

This Policy applies to all personal data that can be obtained from a personal data subject who is a party to a civil law contract with the Company (including an authorized representative of such a party) or a client and / or potential client of the Company wishing to conclude a retail contract with the Company. purchase and sale and / or other civil law contract, as well as those who wish to receive information on the website

PRINCIPLES OF PERSONAL DATA PROCESSING

Personal data is stored and processed in compliance with the requirements of the legislation in a form that allows the PD Subject to be identified, no longer than the purpose of their processing requires, except for cases when the period is set by the contract, the party to which, the beneficiary or the guarantor to which the PD Subject is.

The Company considers personal data as a valuable asset and takes necessary and sufficient measures to protect personal data from unauthorized or accidental access, destruction, modification, blocking, copying, distribution, as well as from other illegal actions.

PURPOSES OF COLLECTING AND PROCESSING PERSONAL DATA

Regulation of the employee's labor relations with the Company (ensuring compliance with laws and other regulatory legal acts, assisting employees in employment, training and promotion, ensuring the personal safety of employees, monitoring the quality of work performed and ensuring the safety of property);

Providing information of a notification or marketing nature, including about new products, services, ongoing marketing campaigns and events (for which there is a prior consent of the PD Subject to receive them);

The volume and nature of the processed personal data, methods of processing personal data correspond to the purposes of processing personal data of PD Subjects.

The Company does not use the personal data of PD Subjects for the purpose of causing them property and moral damage, making it difficult for them to exercise their rights and freedoms.

CATEGORIES OF PERSONAL DATA SUBJECTS

The Company processes personal data of the following categories of PD Subjects:

  1. clients - individuals registered on the Company's Website and purchasing goods, works (services) on the Company's Website, as well as the clients' children;
  2. founders, participants (shareholders, partners, shareholders), managers, representatives, officials and other employees of counterparties - legal entities, as well as individual entrepreneurs;
  3. employees and related close relatives, as well as PD Subjects who are candidates for employment in the Company;
  4. beneficiaries, beneficial owners of customers;
  5. current and former employees of the Company;
  6. participants (shareholders, partners, shareholders), affiliates of the Company, managers of clients who are affiliated with the Company.

OBLIGATIONS OF THE COMPANY

Take the necessary legal, organizational and technical measures or ensure their adoption to protect personal data from unauthorized or accidental access to them, destruction, modification, blocking, copying, provision, dissemination of personal data, as well as from other illegal actions in relation to personal data.

In case of confirmation of the fact of inaccuracy of personal data and on the basis of information provided by the PD Subject or his representative, or the authorized body for the protection of the rights of personal data subjects, clarify the personal data of the Subject, or ensure their clarification (if the processing of personal data is carried out by another person acting on behalf of the Company ) within seven working days from the date of submission of such information and remove the blocking of personal data.

In case of revealing illegal processing of personal data carried out by the Company or a person acting on behalf of the Company, within a period not exceeding three working days from the date of this identification, the Company undertakes to stop the illegal processing of personal data or to ensure that the illegal processing of personal data by the person acting on behalf of Society. If it is impossible to ensure the legality of the processing of personal data, the Company, within a period not exceeding ten working days from the date of detection of the illegal processing of personal data, undertakes to destroy such personal data or ensure their destruction.
