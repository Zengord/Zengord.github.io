smart watch. This actual device fully meets the requirements of a modern person who cannot imagine life without mobile gadgets. With a smartwatch, you can avoid taking a bulky smartphone for your morning run. They are considered more accurate, their error is plus / minus 20 seconds per month. There are models in which a double display of time is provided: with the help of numbers and with the help of arrows.
